OUR PROGRAM

Our 9-month program is divided into 12 modules. The last module is a 6-weeks intensive Final Project where students work on a real project with a real partner. Our training takes place at Epitech (Brussels) every Sunday. During the week, students are supported online in their self-study, homework and peer learning.

We graduated over 100 people in Belgium and more than 300 globally. In Belgium, over 70% found and retained a high-quality job as a developer.

Our core values

Community

Our program is adapted to suit people with different backgrounds, often in vulnerable situations. We teach on Sundays to allow everyone to take control of their personal lives during the week.

Diversity & inclusiveness

We’re largely dependent on volunteers.That’s what makes us an engaged grassroots community, as our students call family

Commitment

Our program is free of charge, although we ask for strong commitment during our 7 month-journey. And you know what? many alumni stay actively involved in the project

Open

Sharing is caring. Our curriculum is 100% open. We believe in knowledge for the many, not the few. Any knowledge is welcome
